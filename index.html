Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your
sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your
favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices
so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our
simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete
control over how you manage your favourite sites.
